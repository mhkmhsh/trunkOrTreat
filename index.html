"Trunk or Treat" is a community Halloween event where participants decorate their vehicle trunks and gather in a designated area, often a parking lot. Children, dressed in costumes, walk from car to car collecting candy and treats. This fun alternative to traditional trick-or-treating provides a safe and festive environment, fostering community spirit. Families can enjoy themed decorations, games, and sometimes even activities like face painting, making it a popular choice for neighborhoods and organizations looking to celebrate Halloween together.
